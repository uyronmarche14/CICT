# Public Content Data Requirements

This file tracks the real data needed to keep the public CICT pages accurate without using placeholder copy or fake actions.

## Contact And Messages

- Official receiving office name.
- Official email address, phone number, office hours, room, building, and campus.
- Contact form owner or reviewer group.
- Expected response time for inquiries.
- Inquiry categories approved for public use.
- Admin retention rule for submitted messages.
- Escalation path for urgent student, event, technical, and partnership concerns.

Required inquiry fields:

- Full name.
- Email address.
- Optional contact number.
- Role or user type.
- Subject.
- Inquiry type.
- Message.
- Status: `new`, `read`, or `archived`.

## Academics

- Official program names for BS Computer Science and BS Information Systems.
- Short program descriptions.
- Program outcomes or graduate profiles.
- Curriculum links or downloadable references.
- Admission or advising contact for each program.
- Any current accreditation, recognition, or department notes.

## Admissions

- Current application period and deadline.
- Required documents.
- Eligibility rules.
- Admission process steps.
- Testing or interview requirements, if any.
- Office contact for applicants.
- Links to official university admissions resources.

## Student Life

- Active student organization list.
- Organization descriptions and adviser/moderator data.
- Regular student activities and event categories.
- Membership process and requirements.
- Campus involvement opportunities.
- Student support services related to CICT.

## Organizations

For each organization:

- Organization ID, acronym, full name, and description.
- Logo, banner, and brand colors.
- Mission, vision, values, and tagline.
- Adviser or moderator contact.
- Official email and social links.
- Office or meeting location.
- Membership requirements, benefits, and join steps.
- Programs, flagship events, committees, partners, and achievements.
- Public officers and member data approved for display.

## Homepage

- Final homepage value proposition for CICT.
- Confirmed benefit labels for the hero area.
- Real development team names, roles, photos, and approved links.
- Current featured news, announcements, events, and updates rules.
- Approved homepage CTA labels and destinations.

## Footer And Social Links

- Official college social links.
- Links that should be hidden until official URLs exist.
- Updated services/about/admissions/contact destinations.
- Copyright and ownership copy.

## Content Ownership

- Page owner for each public route.
- Person responsible for annual review.
- Last reviewed date.
- Source of truth for updates.
- Approval workflow for publishing changed public copy.
