# CICT Website Business Model

## 1. Business Overview

The CICT Website is primarily an institutional platform, not a direct commercial SaaS product.

Its first value is operational and strategic:

- improve communication,
- improve department branding,
- improve public visibility,
- improve organization presentation,
- and reduce manual website maintenance.

The platform creates value by supporting the department’s public image, information flow, and administrative efficiency.

---

# 2. Value Model

## Internal Institutional Value

The website helps CICT:

- publish updates faster,
- keep public information current,
- present organizations professionally,
- make events easier to promote,
- reduce repeated manual content work,
- centralize administrative content management.

## Student Value

Students benefit by having:

- one place for updates,
- clearer organization information,
- accessible event listings,
- visible member and officer pages,
- better department presentation.

## Faculty and Staff Value

Faculty and staff benefit by having:

- a platform for publishing official information,
- less dependency on developers for routine updates,
- organized department content management.

## Branding and Reputation Value

The platform improves:

- department professionalism,
- digital presence,
- consistency of public communication,
- visibility of achievements and activities.

---

# 3. Stakeholder Packaging

## CICT Department Leadership

### Value

- central official website
- stronger department branding
- better content oversight
- more consistent public presence

## Admin Staff and Content Managers

### Value

- secure admin access
- easier publishing workflows
- content management without code editing
- role-based access control

## Student Organizations

### Value

- dedicated organization visibility
- officer and member presentation
- better promotion of activities

## Students and Visitors

### Value

- easier access to department information
- clearer event and organization browsing
- more modern website experience

---

# 4. Sustainability Model

Even if the product is not monetized like SaaS, it still needs sustainability.

## Sustainability Factors

- maintainable content workflows
- easy admin updates
- reusable structure for multiple years
- clear role ownership
- scalable architecture for future modules

## Long-Term Sustainability Value

The platform reduces long-term maintenance cost by making routine updates manageable through the admin panel.

---

# 5. Future Expansion Value

If the platform later expands beyond CICT, possible future positioning could include:

- reusable college/department website platform,
- school organization management platform,
- academic event and announcement platform,
- internal school content management system.

This is not the main current business goal, but the structure supports future productization if desired.

---

# 6. Success Indicators

The value of the platform can be measured through:

- more frequent content updates
- better organization visibility
- easier admin operations
- higher engagement with news and event pages
- more complete and updated member information
- stronger department digital presence

---

# 7. Final Business Statement

The CICT Website creates value not by direct selling in its current form, but by improving institutional communication, visibility, efficiency, and digital professionalism.

If expanded later, it can also become a reusable school or department web platform model.
